The Themes :


The themes used in anime are almost the same that are being used around the world. It includes the themes of friendships, fantasy, love and
many others in the story. What makes it so unique is that how the story was set up and how the author brings the joy or thrill of watching them. Despite the cliche or the
overused themes, combined with its drawing style and execution, it always appear to be distinctive to another type of animation out there.

As for example, both Scooby Doo series and Detective Conan series were having the same detective and solving a mystery themes,
but the execution were done differently. Scooby Doo often tells a story where the team solve the mystery in more straight forward investigation kinf of way, while keep on to please the viewer with comedic themes.
As for Detective Conan, the author puts a sense of adventure in solving the mystery. The character pulls up different deduction theory, solving riddles,
investigating and eliminating list of suspects all while facing a real threat from the criminal. Both of the series were aim towardsa group of viewer which consisting mainly
kids at pre-teenage age, yet anime manage to bring a deeper themes and adventure towards the viewer.

Other than that, anime also willing to bring about heavier themes that were likely more focused on much more mature society, a theme that are not suitable for children as such
betrayal, marriage, drugs, politics, social problems, slavery, war, rape, murder, suicide and so on. While doing this, the industry lost its young viewer, but gaining more adult viewer on the way,
indirectly increase the popularity of anime among working society.
